Tuned Global // White-Label Streaming Music Applications

Project Type

UX, Design, Product Owner

Overview

This project aimed to bring a simple, fast-to-market music appliation product for music labels, telecoms, and brands. The final product was launched with customers in India (Vodafone-Idea), Malaysia, Indonesia, Senegal, and Myanmar.

Top Research & Mapping Final Product

🔎 Research & Mapping

Having previous experience with music streaming mobile applications at JB Hi-Fi I had fair understanding of the task ahead, although the need to bring this project to mutiple asian markets presented a huge challenge.

Once a full feature list had been documented and the basic application flow was decided, clickable prototypes for user testing and sales presentations was created. Through these trials, the following features and restrictions evolved:

Features & Restrictions

  1. Rebrandable

    Each client required custom logo, splash, colour scheme, and unique music curation.

  2. Translatable

    Textual UI elements must be short, simple to translate with universal icon comprehension.

  3. Simple

    UX must have as little onboarding and be as intuitive as possible.

  4. Fast

    Startup and music buffering must be invisible and any delays to be as well-handled as possible.

  5. Full Featured

    All the expected music app features of the time.

  6. Light Footprint

    Install size and music cache must be minimal and user manageable.

  7. Low Overhead

    Must work on the most common low-end mobile devices.

🛠 Final Product

The end result was sold into mutiple countries, and continues to be a valuable product in Tuned Global toolkit.

The entire project included the following:

  • Custom designed UI icon font
  • New internationalised music meta-data CMS
  • Stress-tested to 11,000 concurrent users
  • Ability for authorised publishers to record and insert live video into dynamic playlists.
