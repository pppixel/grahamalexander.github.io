Bio

Hi! Thank you for visiting my portfolio. My name is Graham Alexander. I'm currently the Chief Experience Officer (Global) at Infomo, creating marketing SaaS platforms for advertising agencies and global media owners.

I've earned a reputation for bringing clarity to complex problems, and making good products great. I love to work on meaningful projects with creative, passionate people.

My career so far has provided strong roots in large information systems, developing platforms for government, retail, web & mobile gaming, streaming mobile music & video products, eBook marketplaces and finally ad/marketing-tech.

With an education in art & design, I still draw and paint, make games, and take macro & astro photography whenever the sky's clear.
